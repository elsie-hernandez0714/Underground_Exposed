What we are all about

When Underground Exposed was created we had only one mission in mind, our mission was to bring underground and local artists and expose them to the spotlight. Sometimes amazing talent goes unoticed for years and we wanted to break that cycle. We have Sponsors with whom we work with to have these artists play at local shows and record their first album.

Here at Under ground exposed we know that music can be so powerful and has changed lives and what better way then to bring talent who deserves it. We are all about the music movement. If you don't have an account, what are you waiting for? It's free! All we ask for is when you make it big, to make a small donation to help pay it forward.
